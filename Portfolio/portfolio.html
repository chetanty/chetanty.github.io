Javelin Power Saver

Demo

Made by Chetan Tyagi

Runner-Up of the first MecSimCalc Hackathon, this project made in Python aims to conserve the appliances’ energy, taking decisions to buy a new appliance and therefore reduce electricity expenses. With the help of this easy-to-use app, the user will be able to calculate the electricity consumption of appliances per month, total money you spend on them in a month, compare user’s electricity bill with the country’s average and compare the appliances with popular products in the same range.
